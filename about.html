About Us

Empowering students to collaborate, learn, and contribute through open source.

Our Mission

SPU GitHub on Campus is a student-led community at St. Paul’s University. We believe in learning by doing, sharing knowledge, and collaborating on real-world projects. Our mission is to help students grow as developers, build portfolios, and contribute to open source.
